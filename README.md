# CarburApp: Smart Savings at the Pump
CarburApp is designed to help you find the lowest fuel prices in your area, ensuring you never overpay for a refill again. With a simple and intuitive interface, it puts real-time savings right in your pocket.

# What can you do with CarburApp?
- **Find the Best Deals**: Instantly discover the cheapest gas stations nearby and compare prices on the fly.
- **Interactive Maps**: Visualize stations on a map to see exactly where they are and the easiest way to reach them.
- **Plan Your Trip**: Use the route planning feature to find the best places to refuel along your journey.
- **Save Your Favorites**: Keep a list of your preferred stations for quick access to their current prices.
- **Speak Your Language**: The app is fully translated into English, Italian, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, and Polish.
- **Modern Look**: The interface automatically adapts to your phone's light or dark mode for a comfortable viewing experience at any time of day.
